ChordGAN

Adversarial Perturbations for Image Privacy

creates slight adjustments to photosto makes them illegible to facial detection algorithms.
ensemble method, combining geometric approaches with GAN masking.
see github and try it out here.

web app that prompts users to rate the relevance of various search results.

CameraH

"every day, upload a photo of you visiting a new location around harvard."
